Quire — style guide

Derived from docs/design-feel.md: calm, deliberate, editorial. A worktable for gathered leaves, not a feed to swipe through. This page is the tokens in tokens.css and the recipes in components.css, made visible.

Color — surfaces & ink
Color — gesture & state semantics

Muted on purpose. A drift flag is a routine, high-recall screen result — not an alarm — so it gets an ochre "noted," not a red siren. Accept/defer/reject are three distinct, equally calm hues; none reads as more "correct" than the others.

Typography — families

A literary serif carries judgment text written by a human-facing surface (the wordmark, the card's direction summary) — it should read as written, not computed. A humanist sans carries fast-scan UI. Monospace is reserved for IDs and other literal, technical strings.

Radius & elevation

Soft but not bubbly — bound-paper edges, not app-icon corners. Shadows are a paper resting on a desk, not a glossy floating panel.
